THE Sculpture park

Permanent

Architecture, art and nature come together in Trapholt’s sculpture park.

Set in a scenic location overlooking the Kolding Fjord, Trapholt’s sculpture park offers a stunning extension of your museum visit. The sculpture park presents a collection of monumental works that relate directly to the surrounding architecture and nature.

Here you can explore works by Danish sculptors such as Bent Sørensen, Ingvar Cronhammar, Ole Christensen, Erik Heide, Søren Jensen, Bjørn Nørgaard, Lars Ravn and Finn Reinbothe.

The sculpture park is always open to the public; no tickets required.

The museum guides are happy to provide a plan of the sculpture park, allowing you to explore the works yourself.
